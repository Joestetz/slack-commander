Slack Commander

Slash commands for Slack to keep your team both productive and engaged

A user interface will be coming soon. For now, check out the Setup page to learn how to get set up for use with your team.
Currently available commands:
  • Tasker - A shared checklist for your team
  • Timer - A countdown timer that keeps your channel updated
  • Beer - Tell everyone what beer you're drinking right now!
  • JIRA - Commands for integrating with JIRA
